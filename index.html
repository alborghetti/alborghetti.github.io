Francesco Alborghetti

Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication

SAP DATAMANAGEMENT CONSULTANT

Francesco is co-founder and SAP Data Management Consultant at Inquaero S.r.l.
The consulting company has been established end of 2017 by a team of professional consultants, formerly employed by SAP, where they gained a vast competence in data management and data migration areas.
Our focus is on SAP systems, whether for optimizing the current business flows, or for increasing performance and readiness grade in order to switch to the available "state-of-the-art" ERP solutions (S/4HANA in primis). Our analysis casts light on common patterns, and unveils latent inefficiencies, such as data redundancy (duplicates) and data obsolescence, that is data no longer needed nor queried by end users.
TCO reduction and optimal preparation for upgrading and migrating to newer platform are our mantra.
Our distinctive Data Management solutions consist in:

  • In-depth Analysis of SAP Systems, by Inquaero™ Semplice
  • GDPR compliance and Pseudonymization of relevant business data, by Inquaero™ Anonimo
  • Business Transformation 360-package (SLO,M&A, carve-out, fast data migration, S/4HANA Readiness Check), performed by Inquaero™ Veloce
